# Better Sulfur Cubes

**Better Sulfur Cubes** adds new custom archetypes to the Sulfur Cube, each triggered by
equipping a specific block in its body slot. Each archetype gives the mob a unique behavior that reacts to its surroundings: collisions, surfaces, and the environment.

---

## Archetypes

### Adhesive - *Honey Block*

![Adhesive archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/41ce0759606528952ced972efc64f1d41d57e08d.png)

A Sulfur Cube wearing a **Honey Block** will stick to any wall or ceiling it crashes into.

Unsticks when hit by a player/mob, hooked with a fishing rod, leashed or when the block it was clinging to is broken.

---

### Explosive - *TNT*

![Explosive archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/4eee38f6c4522c0603a2aaf68187abf72178e526.png)

A Sulfur Cube wearing **TNT** becomes a living bomb. It detonates on hard impact against a
wall or the ground, when touching fire or lava, or when struck by a **flaming arrow**.

On explosion, it splits into **two smaller Sulfur Cubes**, just like a natural death... but
with a bang.

---

### Musical - *Note Block*

![Musical archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/6700bea50f91f04109cc0cc3e821003c2a7479d6.png)

A Sulfur Cube wearing a **Note Block** turns every collision into music. When it lands on a
surface, hits a wall, or bounces off a ceiling, it plays the **Note Block instrument** of the
block it collided with, at a random pitch.

---

### Twinkle - *Redstone Lamp / Copper Bulb*

![Twinkle archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0e789ac3e1b664aa40b7a4c29a3e2f378e5ed292.png)

A Sulfur Cube wearing a **Redstone Lamp** or any **Copper Bulb** (including oxidized variants)
toggles between **lit and unlit** on every collision. When lit, its texture changes accordingly.

---

### Redstone - *Redstone Block*

![Redstone archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/3e4dec395e08e954d4faa36865c7e21c18a045fc.png)

A Sulfur Cube wearing a Redstone Block acts as a mobile power source. It emits a strong Redstone signal to the block it is currently standing on and updates all adjacent neighbors as it moves.

---

### Painful - *Magma Block / Cactus*

![Painful archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/fbe6307ec59fdd960269336fa874e962612c25a3.png)

This Sulfur Cube is dangerous. Any mob or player that collides with this cube will take contact damage.

---

### Solid - *Purpur Blocks*

![Solid archetype](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/2ae37f6fecb17741e87f1db636bd6f2ef4629a82.png)

The Sulfur Cube turns completely solid, allowing players to stand and walk on top of it as if it were a real block. The only way to move this Sulfur Cube is by hitting it.

---

## Configuration

All archetypes can be individually **enabled or disabled** by editing `config/better-sulfur-cubes.json` directly.

---

## Requirements

- **Minecraft** 26.2-snapshot-4
- **Fabric Loader** ≥ 0.19.2
- **Fabric API**
